Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Harvestly mobile application (the "Service"). Your privacy is our top priority. As an individual developer, I have built this app to be functional without compromising your personal data.

1. Data Collection & Usage

We believe that your data belongs to you. To that end:

  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account or sign in to use the Service.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

2. Calendar Access

Harvestly may request permission to write to your device's calendar. This access is used solely to create harvest reminder events so you know when your plants are ready.

Calendar data is written directly to your on-device calendar and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. This permission is entirely optional — you can use all other app features without granting calendar access.

3. Garden & Plant Data

Any garden plans, planted items, and harvest tracking data you create within the app are stored exclusively on your device using Apple's built-in storage system.

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

4. Location Data

"Our app requests access to your location to provide [Specific Feature, e.g., plant heartiness zones]. This location data is optional, processed locally on your device, and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties."

Access to your location is strictly optional. If granted, the coordinates are used only within the app session to enable core features. We do not store a history of your location or use it for tracking/advertising.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party analytics (like Google Analytics or Firebase) or advertising SDKs. No data is shared with any third party.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately upon being posted on this page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact me at: idlers.tubes.4u@icloud.com
